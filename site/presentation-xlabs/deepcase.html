VMware Intelligent Assist

I would explore the following possibilities:

  • Can the VMware's chatbot be pro-active, instead of reactive?
  • Can the chatbot be accessible on multiple platforms? For example: NSX Northstar, Slack and VSCode?
  • Can the chatbot talk to more than 1 person at a time?
  • How should chat history and transcript be organized and managed?
  • How can we make this scale to other VMware products?
  • Can the model suggest remediation steps which can be reviewed and approved before being presented to the customer?
