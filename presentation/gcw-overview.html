Genie Conversational UI

To reduce the burden on human agents, Genie was developed as a deterministic chatbot.

In 2021, I designed the standard conversation flow and UI used by all VMware colleagues to interact with Genie across all support channels.

Note: Genie was developed before LLMs gained mainstream adoption, so it does not have advanced language understanding and response generation capabilities.
