Genie's Backstory

Genie was originally built as a virtual assistant for employees.

But, due to a lack of effective product management, adoption was very low.

The core Genie team was disbanded and further development was abandoned.

Today

During the pandemic, it was resurrected as a chatbot.

Today, most colleauges know Genie as the chatbot on Source.

I took on the role of lead designer for Genie in 2021.

First task was to design a new UI layer for the colleague-facing chat widget.
