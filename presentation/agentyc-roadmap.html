Future Roadmap

  • For non-technical users: define variety of practical use-cases that require multi-agent orchestration and provide pre-defined Agents and prompts
  • For developers: integrate with other agentic frameworks to function as a rapid agentic framework comparison tool
  • Replace Python script-based Skills with MCP servers for advanced and stable tool use with predictable outputs
