SwiftUI Development

Adaptive and dynamically themed UI: Leveraging SwiftUI's powerful styling and theming capabilities, including view modifiers, custom style protocols, and environment values. For example: dynamic themes for each Model Provider.

Model-View-ViewModel architecture with a Service Layer: Agentyc primarily follows the MVVM pattern, which is strongly encouraged by SwiftUI, enhanced with a separate Service Layer (DatabaseManager, DockerManager and SessionManager classes) to handle complex business logic and external interactions.

Effective use of Swift's modern concurrency features (async/await, Task) within ObservableObject classes to perform long-running or background tasks without blocking the UI. Progress updates and results are fed back to the UI via @Published properties.
