Origin Story & Product Goals

Microsoft's Autogen Studio

  • Requires technical knowledge to install
  • Lack of streaming responses and real-time updates
  • Lack of out-of-box support for models other than OpenAI's GPT-4o
  • Unintuitive flow for creating Agents, Models, Workflows etc

I took on the challenge of creating an alternative to Autogen Studio to empower non-technical users to experiment with AI Agents and build the right mental model of an AI Agent ecosystem.

Goals for Agentyc

Drastically simplify the installation & onboarding process specifically for non-technical users
Craft workflows to help users develop the right mental model of an AI Agent ecosystem
Provide basic, ready-to-use Skills for web searching and browsing using open source libraries
Support interoperability with AutoGen Studio to take advantage of its community features
