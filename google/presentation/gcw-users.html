Users & Stakeholders

Through stakeholder interviews, I identified 3 groups of users & their needs.

VMware Colleague

User Needs

  • Quick & accurate responses to my support queries.
  • Intuitive and accessible UI to converse with Genie or human agents.

Plan of Action

  • Introduce Genie and build trust with users.
  • Design intuitive, accessible and responsive UI.

Domain Admin

User Needs

  • Guidance and support for managing my channel.
  • Access to user feedback to improve response quality.

Plan of Action

  • Establish standard process for channel onboarding and management.
  • Provide user feedback data to help improve their channel's performance.

Service Director

User Needs

  • Reduce average time-to-respond to colleagues.
  • Ability to track metrics for my service channel

Plan of Action

  • Provide detailed reports of channel activity.
  • Recurring check-ins to understand challenges and changing needs.
