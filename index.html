About Me

As a Senior Product Designer at VMware, I own the end-to-end user experience of products across a variety of domains. For every product, my role involves...

  • Collaborating with Product Managers and other cross-functional stakeholders to influence strategy.
  • Conducting user research in parternship with UX Researchers to understand user needs & challenges.
  • Leading a team of designers to create pixel-perfect mockups.
  • Conducting usability tests with end users using interactive prototypes.
  • Creating design system components to supplement VMware's Clarity design system.
  • Partnering with Developers to bring the concepts to life, providing production-ready HTML & CSS when necessary.
  • Facilitating workshops for brainstorming or prioritizing.
  • Evangelizing importance of UX and helping teams adopt a user-centered design process.
