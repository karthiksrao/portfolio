About Me

As a Senior Product Designer at VMware, I own the end-to-end user experience of products across a variety of domains. For every product, my role involves...

  • Collaborating with Product Managers to influence strategy.
  • Engaging UX Researchers to understand user needs & challenges.
  • Facilitating workshops for brainstorming or prioritizing.
  • Leading a team of designers to create pixel-perfect mockups and test interactive prototypes with end users.
  • Partnering with Developers to bring the concepts to life.
  • Evangelizing importance of UX and helping teams adopt a user-centered design process.
