Allow me to present my concept for Licensed Characters in Chai AI.

Introducing
LICENSED CHARACTERS

Licensed Characters are created by brands, celebrities or influencers to engage their audience on Chai AI.

To distinguish this from community-created characters, brands should be able to upload relevant data to make the character authentic.

How It Works

To facilitate this, Chai AI will have to build a dedicated character-management platform that provides complete control to brands/creators for managing the entire lifecycle of their characters: from uploading propreitary data to viewing engagement & ROI analytics.

For example, to create a character from a movie or tv show, the movie studio would upload a bio and screenplay or script from the movie. Licensed Characters will use models trained on that data to engage in conversations that are informed by the movie plot, dialogues etc.

In another example, a brand like Porsche could upload technical specifications of each of their cars so characters can engage in technical conversations that are backed by accurate data.

Value Proposition

For Users

  • Engage with their favorite celebrities, influencers, brands, movies, tv shows etc in a unique way.
  • Indulge in their favorite movies or tv shows by having a faux-real conversation with fictional characters.

For Brands

  • Leverage this direct line of communication with their audience to promote products, deals or events within the context of a conversation.
  • As Chai AI capabilities evolve, scale this into a personalized brand concierge for every customer.

For Chai AI

  • Add a revenue stream by charging brands to access the platform to create and manage Licensed Characters.
  • Attract new users to the platform. Restrict Licensed Characters to Premium members only, providing another incentive to upgrade.
