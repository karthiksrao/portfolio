What are Official Characters?

Official Characters are created by brands, celebrities or influencers to engage their audience on Character AI.

To facilitate this, Character AI will have to build a dedicated character-management platform that provides complete control to brands/creators for managing the entire lifecycle of their characters: from providing data for fine-tuning models to viewing engagement & ROI analytics.

Benefits

For Users

  • Engage with their favorite celebrities, influencers, brands, movies, tv shows etc in a unique way.
  • Indulge in their favorite movies or tv shows by having a faux-real conversation with fictional characters.

For Brands

  • Leverage this direct line of communication with their audience to promote products, deals or events within the context of a conversation.
  • As C.AI capabilities evolve, turn this into a personalized brand concierge for every customer.

For Character AI

  • Add revenue stream by charging brands to access the platform to create and manage Official Characters.
  • Attract new users to the platform. Restrict Official Characters to C.AI+ members only, providing another incentive to upgrade.

The Big Picture

By comparing Character AI's current experience to the competition, I identified the following opportunities for improving Character AI:

  • Facilitate users in building a personal connection to characters, which will increase user retention and new user adoption.
  • Discover more opportunities for monetization or added benefits for C.AI+ users, including consumables.
  • Design a seamless and consistent experience across all platforms to support higher modalities, including new and niche platforms such as the Apple Vision Pro.
  • Unlock use cases that provide more than entertainment value, which would make Character AI irreplacable to its users.
